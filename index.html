Approach

How I deliver reliable
data systems.

01

Understand

I start with the business question. Who uses the data, what decisions does it support, and what does “correct” look like? The architecture follows from the answers.

02

Design

I model the data, choose the right tools for the problem, and plan the pipeline end-to-end before writing code. No over-engineering, no under-engineering.

03

Build & Test

I build iteratively with data quality tests at every stage. Schema validation, row counts, referential integrity — failures surface immediately, not three months later.

04

Ship & Document

I containerise, automate, and document everything. Your team inherits a system they can maintain, extend, and trust long after the engagement ends.
